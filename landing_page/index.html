Who We Are

Our goal and mission at MyJournalCRM is position ourselves as the solution leader for helping fellow micro-entrepreneurs and network marketers to maximize their businesses. In order to do that, we’ve consulted with several successful network marketers to understand what information is essential and important, patented algorithms that track everything in the direct sales lifecycle, and unlike most other generic online CRMs, we focus on the whole suite of tools and data for our members – not just prospecting. We want to maximize productivity and efficiency while minimizing the time wasted on micro-managing and tracking data, progress, and productions. This ultimately frees you up to be the best marketer possible without becoming a micro-manager.
